Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 7, 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

HMI2.ai, Inc. ("HMI2.ai", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. Roadtrip-Copilot has been designed from the ground up to be a privacy-first application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile applications, websites, and services (collectively, the "Services").

The Core Principle: On-Device Processing

Our fundamental privacy promise is that **all of your sensitive personal data is processed and stored directly on your device.** This includes:

  • Your precise location and location history.
  • Your user preferences and interaction history.
  • The content of your voice commands.
This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it or use a cloud-based feature.

Information We Collect

We collect a minimal amount of information to provide and improve our Services:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect basic information such as your email address and a hashed password.
  • Payment Information: If you purchase roadtrips, our third-party payment processor (e.g., Apple, Google, Stripe) will handle the transaction. We do not store your full credit card information.
  • Creator Economy Data: If you participate in our first-discovery program, we collect information necessary to attribute content to you and to calculate and distribute revenue share. This includes anonymized discovery data and your creator profile information.
  • Anonymous Analytics (Optional): If you choose to opt-in, we collect anonymized and aggregated usage data to help us understand how our Services are used and to improve them. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Services.
  • Process transactions and manage your account.
  • Administer the Creator Economy and revenue sharing program.
  • Communicate with you about your account and our Services.
  • Understand and analyze how you use our Services (only if you opt-in to analytics).

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent.
  • With third-party service providers who help us operate our Services (e.g., payment processors), who are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
  • To comply with legal obligations, such as a court order or government request.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of HMI2.ai, our users, or the public.

Your Data Rights and Choices

You have control over your data. Within the Privacy Center in the app, you can:

  • Manage your location data settings.
  • Opt-in or out of personalized recommendations and anonymous analytics.
  • Export a copy of your account data.
  • Delete your account and all associated data permanently.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our website and in our mobile application.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@hmi2.ai.
